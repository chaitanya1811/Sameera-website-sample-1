About Us

Meet the League Behind the Prep

At College Prep League, we're not your average counselors. We're writers, researchers, marketers, and mathematicians—brought together by a shared mission: helping ambitious students write unforgettable college applications.

We've read the fine print of college admissions, lived the student life on global campuses, and mentored hundreds through the messy middle of self-doubt, deadlines, and drafts. And we love it.

Here's who's coaching you behind the scenes:

Prabha Sethy

Founder & Senior Mentor

Prabha Sethy is an educator who has spent over three decades shaping academic journeys. She runs a highly respected mathematics coaching centre and is the author of several mathematics books published by HarperCollins. Her mentorship is rooted in clarity, discipline, and a deep understanding of how young minds learn and thrive.

With her vast experience working with students across academic levels, she brings unparalleled insight into guiding students not just through the rigor of curriculum, but through the more nuanced challenges of preparing for college. Prabha combines structure with empathy, helping students unlock their academic and personal potential.

Samira Sethy

College Counselor & Strategy Lead

A Kelley School of Business alum who's worn many hats—marketer, tutor, and now, counselor—Samira helps students see themselves through a storytelling lens.

She believes a great application is really great marketing: authentic, sharp, and impossible to ignore. With a marketer's mind and a mentor's heart, she teaches students how to market themselves—without ever sounding like a pitch deck.

At College Prep League, Samira focuses on helping students articulate their stories with precision and confidence. From extracurricular planning to essay development, she ensures each student presents a thoughtful, authentic, and strategic profile that stands out to admissions committees.

Priyanka Sethy

Academic Advisor & Writing Specialist

Priyanka Sethy holds degrees from the University of Chicago and Columbia University, and is currently completing her PhD at Harvard University. A seasoned academic and mentor, she has led workshops on academic writing, research methodology, and creative expression for students across age groups and academic levels.

At College Prep League, Priyanka supports students in building intellectual depth into their applications. Whether guiding a research paper or refining a personal statement, she helps students develop writing that is articulate, insightful, and aligned with the expectations of the world's most competitive universities.

Together, We Are College Prep League.

Three different paths. One shared mission: helping students win at college admissions—with strategy, soul, and a whole lot of support.
